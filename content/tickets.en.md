## How do I get tickets?

We have prepared tickets in several categories for you. If you are leaving on Sunday, please also book a bus ticket, as we do not expect the single public transport service on Sunday to be sufficient for everyone.

Public advance sales begin with the Easterhegg.

Click here for the [Håck ma's Ticket Shop](https://tickets.hack-mas.at/)

## What tickets are available?


* Ticket: €168 Early Bird; once all Early Bird tickets have been sold, the standard ticket price is €178.

* Supporter: With these tickets, you support the event and help fund the Friends ticket.

* Friends: On request, you can obtain a Friends ticket at a reduced price. Please contact us at kontakt@hack-mas.at and let us know how much you can afford. Please bear in mind that we can only offer discounts if other people buy Supporter tickets.

* Merch: T-shirts for €25 and hoodies for €55, fitted or straight cut.

* Shuttle: €12 for the return journey on Sunday evening.

* Caravan: €70
