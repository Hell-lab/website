Never been to Håck ma's Castle before? To make sure you’re fully prepared, we’ve put together a list of things you might need!

## Recommended Packing List

- [ ] Label ALL your belongings
- [ ] Your ticket (in any form)
- [ ] Shampoo (preferably biodegradable)
- [ ] Towel 
- [ ] SWAG
    - [ ] Wearable blinkies
    - [ ] CCC and Chaos clothing
    - [ ] Medieval costume
    - [ ] Functional clothing
    - [ ] Your favourite T-shirt
    - [ ] Discordian hat
    - [ ] And anything else you feel comfortable in
- [ ] Sun protection
- [ ] Sun cream
- [ ] Sleeping bag (for tent, dormitory)
- [ ] Duvet cover and sheet (for room) in the castle
- [ ] Sleeping mat (for tent, dormitory)
    - [ ] Sleeping mat
    - [ ] Camp bed
    - [ ] Inflatable mattress
    - [ ] Pillow
- [ ] 3D sleep mask
- [ ] Earplugs
- [ ] Chargers
- [ ] Power bank
- [ ] Computronium
- [ ] Multi-socket adapter, just in case
- [ ] Sunglasses
- [ ] Jumper/jacket (it can get quite chilly at night)
- [ ] Insect repellent skin spray and clothing spray, just in case
- [ ] Torch (for the campsite)
- [ ] Rucksack for carrying things as you move from room to room 

## Packing list recommendation – optional items
- [ ] Portable outdoor seating
    - [ ] Inflatable air lounger
    - [ ] Camping chair
    - [ ] Folding deckchair
- [ ] LED lighting
- [ ] Art
- [ ] Projects
- [ ] Camping table (to set up your display project, if you wish)
- [ ] Picnic blanket
- [ ] Your favourite toilet paper
- [ ] Outdoor-suitable slippers (e.g. comfortable beach slippers?)
- [ ] Outdoor power cable >=10m (for the chance of having power in the tent)
- [ ] Swimwear / swimming trunks / bikini + beach towel (for the lake)
- [ ] Water bottle

## Anti-packing list – things you definitely won’t need
- [ ] Your own sound system
- [ ] Loads of cash (2 cash machines nearby)
- [ ] Regular food (3 restaurants nearby, plus all-day breakfast at the castle, and Billa and Eurospar next door)
- [ ] Back-up of your devices (you’ll do that, but leave it at home :-> )
