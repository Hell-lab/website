## Travelling by car
If you are travelling by car, there is a short-stay car park which you can use for loading and unloading. There are only a limited number of parking spaces available, and the car park itself is a seven-minute walk away.

##  Travelling by public transport

The nearest bus stop to our castle is Ottenschlag b.Bad Traunstein. Unfortunately, there is very little public transport, particularly at weekends, that takes you directly to the castle; we have therefore arranged a [shuttle]({{< relref "shuttle.md" >}}) on Sundays.
