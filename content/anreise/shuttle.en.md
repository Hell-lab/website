You can travel to and from the venue by public transport on weekdays. You can find the latest timetable on the ÖBB website (Ottenschlag near Bad Traunstein) here: https://fahrplan.oebb.at/webapp/

There is basically no public transport in Ottenschlag on Saturdays and Sundays.

We have therefore organised a shuttle service by coach on the main departure day (Sunday), and you can buy tickets for this on Pretix.

Departure from Ottenschlag (Sunday, 30 August): 17:00 and 20:00.
