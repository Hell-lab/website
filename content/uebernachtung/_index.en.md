## Camping

Camping is once again available right next to the castle on the apple orchard. In summer, the trees will be in full leaf and provide shade for the tents. There will be eco-toilets right next to the camping area. You can use the flush toilets and shower facilities in the castle. 

![A picture of the camping meadow. Numerous apple trees are lined up in rows here. The castle can be seen in the background.](images/camping.png)

You will need your own tent, sleeping mat and sleeping bag. There will be dimmed lighting for the campsite; you are responsible for providing your own light inside the tent. Power to the Tree will be provided.

## Dormitory

The option to stay in a dormitory will be available again this year. There will also be dormitories reserved exclusively for women, agender, inter, trans and non-binary people. You can use the showers (individual cubicles) and washing facilities in the castle.

You will need your own sleeping mat and sleeping bag or your own bedding.

However, due to limited space, we generally recommend sleeping on the apple orchard.

## (limited) Rooms in the castle

There is a limited number of rooms, which are allocated on a needs-based basis (for people with disabilities, families with young children). If you would like one of these rooms, please email us at accessibility@hack-mas.at, stating the reason why you require a room.

You will need to bring your own bed linen. Rooms must be left in a clean and tidy condition; if this is not possible, please let us know when you check in or out.

## (limited) Caravan

There is a limited number of caravan tickets available, which you can purchase with your festival ticket or separately. You will need one ticket per caravan. Every person in the caravan also requires a standard ticket.
